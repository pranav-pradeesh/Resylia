# Quick Start: Admin Team Management

## For Admins: Get Started in 2 Minutes

### Step 1: Access Team Admin
1. Log in to Resylia dashboard
2. Look for **"team admin"** link in top right (orange text)
3. Click it

### Step 2: Invite Your First Team Member
1. Fill in the invite form:
   - **Email**: their@company.com
   - **Role**: Select Employee/Manager/HR
   - **Department**: (optional) e.g., "Engineering"
2. Click **"Send Invite"**
3. They'll get an email with the join link

### Step 3: New Member Joins
1. Member gets email with invite link
2. Clicks link → signs up/logs in
3. Goes through 3-step onboarding (name, department, etc.)
4. Ends up on their dashboard
5. **You'll see them in your Team Members list!**

### Step 4: Manage Your Team
- **Change Role**: Use dropdown next to member name
- **Deactivate**: Click "Deactivate" if they leave
- **View Pending**: Check "Pending Invites" tab
- **Cancel Invite**: Revoke an invite before they accept

## Common Questions

**Q: What's the difference between Employee, Manager, and HR?**
- **Employee**: Can do check-ins, view their own data
- **Manager**: Can see team members' aggregated stats (no individual data)
- **HR**: Can access analytics and team insights
- **Admin**: Can manage team and invites

**Q: Can I change someone's role later?**
Yes! Use the dropdown next to their name on the Team Members tab.

**Q: What if I accidentally send the wrong role?**
1. Cancel the pending invite
2. Send a new one with correct role

**Q: Do I have a limit on team members?**
Yes - based on your plan. If you hit the limit, upgrade your plan.

**Q: What if they don't get the email?**
1. Check their spam folder
2. Cancel the invite and try again

## The Three Tabs

### 👥 Team Members
Shows all active people on your team. 
- Change roles using dropdown
- Deactivate if they leave
- See when they joined

### ⏳ Pending Invites
Shows invites you've sent but they haven't accepted yet.
- Cancel if you change your mind
- Invites expire after 30 days

### ➕ Invite New Member
Form at the top to add new team members.
- Email is required
- Role is required
- Department is optional

---

## Tips & Tricks

✅ **Batch invite**: You can invite multiple people - just send one at a time
✅ **Departments help**: Add departments so the data is organized
✅ **Deactivate, don't delete**: Deactivating keeps their history
✅ **Manager emails**: Optional but helps organize reporting structure
